The Emo Robot

Emo Robot is a special type of robot designed to interact with humans by detecting and responding to emotions.

It is capable of recognizing facial expressions, voice, gestures, and emotions of humans to provide appropriate responses. Emo Robot can help users feel comfortable, reduce stress, and improve mood. Additionally, Emo Robot is also used in research on psychology and linguistics to further understand how humans interact with machines.

The Vector Robot

Vector Robot is a smart home robot developed by Anki. It has the ability to interact with humans by recognizing voices, faces, and gestures.

Vector can perform simple tasks around the house, play games, and interact with users. It also has the ability to learn and adapt to its surrounding environment to create a unique interactive experience for users. Vector is considered one of the smartest and cutest home robots on the market.
